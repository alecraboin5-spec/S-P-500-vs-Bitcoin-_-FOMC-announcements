# Fintech Project: Bitcoin vs S&P 500 Analysis Around FOMC Announcements

## Project Overview
This project aims to analyze the price movements of Bitcoin in relation to the S&P 500 Index around Federal Open Market Committee (FOMC) announcements. By understanding the correlations and market reactions, traders and investors can make more informed decisions.

## Table of Contents
1. Introduction
2. Data Collection
3. Methodology
4. Results

## 1. Introduction
With the growing importance of cryptocurrencies, it is essential to explore how traditional financial markets react during economic announcements. This project examines these dynamics using Bitcoin and the S&P 500 as key indicators.

## 2. Data Collection
Data was collected from various financial data providers for both Bitcoin and S&P 500, focusing on timeframes around FOMC meeting dates.

## 3. Methodology
We employed statistical methods to evaluate the relationship between the two assets, particularly during high-volatility periods surrounding FOMC announcements.

## 4. Results
The analysis aims to highlight significant trends and patterns observed in price movements before and after FOMC announcements.

## 5. Conclusion
In conclusion, this project provides insights into the intertwining of cryptocurrency markets and traditional equity markets, particularly in reaction to federal monetary policy announcements.

## 6. Future Work
Future investigations could involve deeper dives into specific economic indicators and their impact on asset prices.
